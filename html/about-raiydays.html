About us

Rainy Days creates outdoor clothing to help people enjoy nature, from weekend adventures close to home to expeditions. The ever-changing climate has honed our craftsmanship and curiosity over time.


It has also taught us humility, recognizing the equal worth of all people and our interdependence. This motivates our tireless efforts to preserve the planet we all call home.
