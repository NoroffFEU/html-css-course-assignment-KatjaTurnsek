Privacy Policy

Learn how Rainy Days collects, stores, and uses your personal information. Our privacy policy ensures your data is handled securely and with care.

1. Introduction

At Rainy Days, we value your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you visit our website, make purchases, and engage with our services. By using our site, you consent to the collection and use of your information as outlined in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect personal information, such as your name, email address, phone number, shipping address, and payment details, when you create an account, make a purchase, or interact with our website. We may also collect non-personal data, such as browser type, IP address, and browsing activity.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information to process orders, communicate with you about your orders, and improve your shopping experience. We may also use your information for marketing purposes, such as sending promotional emails about our new products and offers, if you have opted in to receive such communications.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. We use encryption and other security measures to ensure the safety of your data. However, no method of online transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your data.

5. Sharing Your Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties except as necessary to complete your transactions (e.g., payment processors and shipping providers). We may also share your data with third-party service providers who assist with website operations, marketing, and analytics.

6. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to improve user experience, track website traffic, and remember user preferences. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal data. If you would like to request a copy of your personal information or make changes, please contact us at customer@rainydays.com.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and we encourage you to review it periodically for updates.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at customer@rainydays.com.
